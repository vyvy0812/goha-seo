# Search Intent Analysis: các địa điểm du lịch đà nẵng

> **Keyword**: các địa điểm du lịch đà nẵng
> **Primary Intent**: Informational (Confidence: High)
> **Micro-Intent**: Know (Travel Discovery/Planning)
> **User Motivation**: The user wants to discover popular and beautiful tourist attractions in Da Nang to plan their trip.
> **Recommended Format**: Comprehensive Guide / Listicle (Top X attractions) with high-quality visuals.
> **SERP Features**: Featured Snippet (List), Image Pack, Map Pack (Local Pack), People Also Ask.

## Intent Breakdown
1. **Primary Intent (Informational)**: Users are searching for a broad overview of what Da Nang has to offer. They want to see "best of" lists, categories (beaches, mountains, landmarks), and specific details about each location.
2. **Secondary Intent (Commercial/Local)**: Users may be comparing locations to decide where to visit first or looking for ticket prices and operating hours. There is a strong local component as these are physical destinations.
3. **Micro-Intent (Know Multi)**: This isn't a "Know Simple" query (like "height of Eiffel Tower"). It requires a multi-faceted answer covering culture, nature, and modern entertainment.

## Content Mapping Requirement
| Intent Found | Mandatory Content Elements |
|--------------|----------------------------|
| **Informational** | Comprehensive Guide, Definitions of landmarks, "How-to get there" steps, High-quality Artistic Images. |
| **Local** | Map citations, Addresses, specific "Heritage Insight" for each location. |

## Heritage Brand Angle
For Heritage Vietnam Airlines, the intent should be shifted from "generic list" to "curated, sophisticated collection". The traveler isn't just looking for "places", they are looking for "experiences" and "cultural bridges".
