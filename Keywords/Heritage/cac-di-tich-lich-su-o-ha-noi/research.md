## 3. Strategic Recommendations
*   **Brand Alignment**: Heritage acts as "The Cultural Bridge." We must not just list these places, but describe their *cultural weight*. For instance, Văn Miếu isn't just a university; it's the enduring symbol of Vietnamese scholarly tradition.
*   **Aviation Subtext**: Subtle tie-ins to arriving in Hanoi via Vietnam Airlines to begin this historical journey.
*   **Content Angle**: Group the sites logically (by era or theme) rather than a random listicle to reflect magazine-level editorial standards.
