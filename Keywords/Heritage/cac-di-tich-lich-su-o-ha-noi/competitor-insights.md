## Strategic Analysis
*   **Common Angles**: Simply listing sites with brief physical descriptions and historical dates.
*   **Content Gaps**: They treat these as separate tourist spots. They lack a cohesive narrative thread explaining *why* Hanoi is considered the city of "ngàn năm văn hiến". They miss out on the deep architectural analysis and the sensory experience of visiting these sites.
*   **Structure Recommendations**:
    *   Instead of a flat list 1-13, we should categorize them thematically (e.g., The Imperial Heritage, Spiritual Sanctuaries, Traces of Resistance). This feels much more like a high-end magazine feature.
    *   We must include a dedicated "Heritage Insight" section for the architectural and cultural depth, satisfying the brand persona.
