# Search Intent Analysis: nghị định 115 an toàn thực phẩm

> **Keyword**: nghị định 115 an toàn thực phẩm
> **Primary Intent**: Informational (Legal/Regulatory Interpretation)
> **Secondary Intent**: Commercial Investigation (B2B Compliance)
> **Micro-Intent**: Know (Complex) - Users aren't just asking for the name of the decree; they need to understand the *implications*, specifically penalty levels and violation types to ensure business compliance.
> **User Motivation**: To identify specific administrative penalties and regulations under Decree 115/2018/NĐ-CP to prevent legal risks and ensure safe food production/trading operations.
> **Recommended Format**: Comprehensive Guide / Regulatory Compliance Analysis (structured with clear headers for fines and violations).
> **SERP Features**: Featured Snippet (Summary of Fines), Knowledge Panel (Decree Info), People Also Ask (Specific fine amounts).
