# Quick Semantic Analysis: Nghị định 115 an toàn thực phẩm

## Top Entities
1.  **Nghị định 115/2018/NĐ-CP** - [Concept/Legal] - The core regulation defining penalties for food safety violations. Replaced Decree 178/2013/NĐ-CP.
2.  **Vi phạm hành chính** - [Concept] - The nature of the risks businesses face (Administrative sanctions, not just warnings).
3.  **Mức phạt tiền** - [Concept] - The primary user pain point. Fines can range from low to 7x the value of goods.
4.  **Giấy chứng nhận cơ sở đủ điều kiện ATTP** - [Certificate] - Lack of this is a common violation with heavy fines.
5.  **Tự công bố sản phẩm** - [Procedure] - Related mechanism (often under Decree 15) but violations regarding it are punished under Decree 115.
6.  **Ghi nhãn hàng hóa** - [Concept] - Incorrect labeling is a frequent violation cited in the decree.
7.  **Phụ gia thực phẩm** - [Ingredient] - Using banned or excessive additives is a severe violation.
8.  **WIN Flavor (R&D Partner)** - [Brand/Solution] - Strategic partner providing compliant ingredients and documentation (COA) to prevent violations at the source.

## Brand Context & Strategic Angle (WIN Flavor)
*   **The "Prevention" Angle**: Most businesses look at Decree 115 when they are *already* in trouble or scared. WIN Flavor positions itself as the partner for *Pre-emptive Compliance*.
*   **R&D Solution**: "Don't just worry about fines; worry about your formula."
    *   Violations regarding **Additives** (Article 5, 6) are severe. WIN Flavor provides *Natural Extracts* and *Permitted Additives* with precise dosage (R&D consultancy).
    *   Violations regarding **Origin/Expiry** (Article 4). WIN Flavor provides clear COA and fresh sourcing.
*   **Tone**: Educational, Warning but Solution-Oriented. "Understanding the law to grow sustainably."
