# Competitor Insights: Nghị định 148 an toàn thực phẩm

## Top 3 Organic Results
All top results are **Official Legal Texts** or **Government Databases**. There is a distinct lack of "Explanatory Articles" or "Business Guides".

### 1. Cổng Thông tin điện tử Chính phủ (vanban.chinhphu.vn)
*   **Type**: Official Gazette (Metadata & PDF).
*   **Content**: Raw legal text, download links.
*   **Structure**: Legal metadata (Signer, Date, Effective Date) -> PDF Download.
*   **User Exp**: Hard to read on mobile. Requires downloading PDF to see content.

### 2. Thư Viện Pháp Luật (thuvienphapluat.vn)
*   **Type**: Legal Database.
*   **Content**: Full raw text of Decree 148/2025/NĐ-CP + Cross-references.
*   **Structure**: Table of Contents (H2: Chương I, II...) -> Articles (H3: Điều 1, 2...).
*   **User Exp**: Comprehensive but overwhelming. Paywall for advanced features (diagrams/English).

### 3. Cổng thông tin Bộ Y tế (moh.gov.vn)
*   **Type**: Government News / Document Repository.
*   **Content**: Announcement of the Decree + Full text.
*   **Structure**: Title (H1) -> Intro -> Document Body.

## Content Gap Analysis
| Competitor Weakness | WIN Flavor Opportunity |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Raw Legal Text**: No summaries or business implications. | **Expert Analysis**: Breakdown "What does this mean for R&D/Export?". |
| **No "How-to"**: Doesn't say *where* to submit files now. | **Actionable Guide**: "Submit self-declaration to [Sở Y tế] instead of [Cục ATTP] for [Product X]". |
| **Dry/Boring**: Pure text, no visuals or diagrams. | **Visual/Engaging**: Use bullet points, bold text, and "Expert Remarks". |

## Structural Recommendations
*   **H1**: Must include "Nghị định 148" but add value (e.g., "Phân tích & Hướng dẫn").
*   **H2 Structure**:
    *   Definition (What is it?)
    *   Key Changes (Central vs Local)
    *   Impact on Business (Export, Testing)
    *   WIN Flavor Solutions (Compliance Support)
*   **Differentiator**: Add a "Comparison Table" (Old vs New Regulation).
