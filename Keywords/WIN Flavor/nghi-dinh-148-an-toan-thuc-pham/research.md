# Research: Nghị định 148 an toàn thực phẩm

> **Context**: WIN Flavor (R&D Partner) | **Intent**: Informational/Compliance Guide
> **Legal Core**: Nghị định 148/2025/NĐ-CP (Decentralization) & Nghị định 15/2018/NĐ-CP (Base)

## 1. Semantic Entities (Entity Context)

### **A. Legal & Regulatory (Core)**
| Entity | Type | Relevance to WIN Flavor |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Nghị định 148/2025/NĐ-CP** | Legal Document | **Primary Topic**. The new decree decentralizing food safety management. Effective July 1, 2025. |
| **Nghị định 15/2018/NĐ-CP** | Legal Document | The foundational decree. Decree 148 modifies the *implementation* (who checks) of Decree 15 (what is checked). |
| **Nghị định 115/2018/NĐ-CP** | Legal Document | Penalties. Decentralization implies local inspectors (Sở Y tế) will now enforce these penalties directly. |
| **Ủy ban nhân dân cấp tỉnh** | Organization | New primary authority for state management of food safety (replacing some Central roles). |
| **Sở Y tế / Sở ATTP** | Organization | The executing agency receiving decentralized powers (certifying exports, State inspection). |
| **Giấy chứng nhận cơ sở đủ điều kiện ATTP** | Concept | Key administrative document. Issuance power shifts to local levels for many cases. |

### **B. Industry & WIN Flavor Scope**
| Entity | Type | Connection |
