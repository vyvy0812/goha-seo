## Strategic Analysis
*   **Common Angles**:
    *   **Focus on Procedures**: FOSI heavily emphasizes the "How-to" (Hồ sơ, Trình tự) for self-declaration.
    *   **Legal Categorization**: Law firms separate the content into Hierarchies (Laws > Decrees > Circulars).
    *   **Direct Access**: LuatVietnam focuses on providing the text and summary/schema of the decree itself.

*   **Content Gaps (Opportunities for WIN Flavor)**:
    *   **No Technical/R&D Depth**: Competitors list laws or procedures but fail to explain *how* ingredient selection impacts compliance (e.g., choosing X additive avoids Y registration).
    *   **Missing "Solution" Context**: They treat compliance as paperwork. WIN Flavor can treat compliance as a **Product Strategy** (Turn-key solution).
    *   **Outdated/Static**: Most content is static. We can differentiate by explicitly mentioning the **2026 Decentralization (Decree 148)**.

*   **Structure Recommendations**:
    *   **Hybrid Approach**: Combine the "Legal Encyclopedia" authority of LuatVietnam with the "Service Walkthrough" of FOSI.
    *   **New Section**: "Why Decree 15 Matters for R&D" (Connecting Law to Flavor/Ingredient choices).
    *   **Actionable Guide**: Break down "Tự công bố" vs "Đăng ký" with clear decision matrices.
