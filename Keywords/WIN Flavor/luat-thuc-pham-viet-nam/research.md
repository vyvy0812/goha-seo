# Quick Semantic Analysis: Luật thực phẩm Việt Nam

## Brand Context (WIN Flavor)
- **Role**: Technology Partner / R&D Solutionist.
- **Problem**: Many F&B startups focus on "tasty" but forget "legal". Incorrect ingredients or dosages can lead to product recall.
- **Solution**: WIN Flavor advises on compliant ingredients and formulation *during* the R&D process, ensuring the final product meets "Luật An toàn thực phẩm".
- **Tone Integration**: "Expert Scientist" warning about legal pitfalls in early R&D.

## Top Entities
1.  **Luật An toàn thực phẩm 55/2010/QH12** - [Law] - The foundation of all food regulations in Vietnam.
2.  **Nghị định 15/2018/NĐ-CP** - [Decree] - The "guidebook" for business implementation (Self-declaration vs. Registration).
3.  **Nghị định 115/2018/NĐ-CP & 124/2021/NĐ-CP** - [Decree] - Penalties for administrative violations (fines up to 7x value of goods).
4.  **Nghị định 43/2017/NĐ-CP & 111/2021/NĐ-CP** - [Decree] - **Mandatory Product Labeling** (Name, Ingredients, Origin, Expiry Dates).
5.  **Thông tư 24/2019/TT-BYT & 17/2023/TT-BYT** - [Circular] - Management of **Food Additives** (Permitted list & ML).
6.  **Cục An toàn thực phẩm (VFA)** - [Organization] - The governing body managing permits and inspections.
7.  **Tự công bố sản phẩm** - [Procedure] - The mechanism for standard pre-packaged foods (simplified procedure).
8.  **Đăng ký bản công bố sản phẩm** - [Procedure] - For health supplements, dietary foods (strict procedure).
9.  **Truy xuất nguồn gốc (Traceability)** - [Concept] - Requirement for raw materials (Circular 17/2021/TT-BNNPTNT).

## Quick Wins (Semantic Injections)
- Mention "Nghị định 15" immediately when talking about product launch.
- Differentiate between "Thực phẩm thường" and "Thực phẩm bảo vệ sức khỏe".
- Link "Phụ gia thực phẩm" to WIN Flavor's compliant ingredient list.
- Use "R&D" as the stage to prevent legal issues (Preventive approach).
