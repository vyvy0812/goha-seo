# Search Intent Analysis: luật thực phẩm việt nam

## 1. Intent Classification
- **Primary Intent**: **Informational** (User seeks to understand the legal framework of food safety in Vietnam).
- **Secondary Intent**: **Commercial Investigation** (Business owners checking compliance requirements).
- **Comparison**:
    - "Search Volume": Medium/High sensitivity for businesses.
    - "Click-Through Probability": High (Users need detailed explanations, not just a quick answer).

## 2. Micro-Intent (Google "Know-Do-Go")
- **Type**: **KNOW** (Complex).
- **Reasoning**: The subject covers multiple decrees, laws, and regulations (Law on Food Safety 2010, Decree 15/2018/ND-CP, etc.). A single snippet cannot answer this. The user needs a comprehensive guide to navigate the legal landscape.

## 3. User Motivation
- **Core Desire**: "I need to know what the current food safety laws in Vietnam are so that I can ensure my business operates legally and avoids penalties."
- **Specific Needs**:
    - Key legal documents (Law No. 55/2010/QH12).
    - Implementing decrees (Decree 15/2018/ND-CP).
    - Penalties for violations.
    - Procedures for obtaining certificates.

## 4. Content Strategy
- **Recommended Format**: **Comprehensive Guide / Pillar Page**.
- **Structure**:
    - **Introduction**: Importance of Food Law in Vietnam.
    - **Core Legal Framework**: The Law on Food Safety (55/2010/QH12) - Key highlights.
    - **Implementing Decrees**: Deep dive into Decree 15/2018/ND-CP (Crucial for businesses).
    - **Compliance Procedures**: Self-declaration vs. Registration.
    - **Penalties**: What happens if you violate?
    - **WIN Flavor's Role**: R&D consultation to ensure product compliance from the formulation stage.
- **Tone**: Professional, Authoritative, yet Simplified for business owners (Grade 5 readability).

## 5. SERP Feature Predictions
- **Featured Snippet**: Likely a definition of "Luật an toàn thực phẩm" or a list of key decrees.
- **People Also Ask**:
    - "Luật an toàn thực phẩm mới nhất là gì?"
    - "Nghị định 15 về an toàn thực phẩm là gì?"
    - "Xin giấy vệ sinh an toàn thực phẩm ở đâu?"
