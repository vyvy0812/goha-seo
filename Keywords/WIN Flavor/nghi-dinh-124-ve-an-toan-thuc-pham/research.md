# Research: Nghị định 124 về an toàn thực phẩm

## Top Entities (From Legal References)
1.  **Nghị định 124/2021/NĐ-CP**:
    -   *Role*: Amends and supplements Decree 115/2018/ND-CP.
    -   *Key Change*: Increases fines (max 100M VND for individuals, 200M VND for orgs, or 7x value of goods) and expands authority.
2.  **Nghị định 115/2018/NĐ-CP**:
    -   *Role*: Base regulation on administrative penalties.
3.  **Hành vi vi phạm về Tự công bố (Self-declaration)**:
    -   *Fine*: 40M - 50M VND for not declaring products (increased range).
4.  **Hàng hóa không rõ nguồn gốc, xuất xứ**:
    -   *Fine*: Increased significantly. confiscated and destroyed.
5.  **Hàng hết hạn sử dụng (Expired goods)**:
    -   *Fine*: Phạt 100% - 200% giá trị hàng hóa (if value > 30M VND).
