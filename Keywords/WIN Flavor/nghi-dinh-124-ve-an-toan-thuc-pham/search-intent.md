# Search Intent Analysis: nghị định 124 về an toàn thực phẩm

## 1. Intent Classification
- **Primary Intent**: **Informational** (User wants to know the content, fines, and changes brought by Decree 124).
- **Secondary Intent**: **Commercial Investigation** (Business owners "fear" fines and look for ways to comply).
- **Comparison**:
    - "Search Volume": High (Businesses checking penalties).
    - "Click-Through Probability": High (Users look for specific "numbers" - how much is the fine?).

## 2. Micro-Intent (Google "Know-Do-Go")
- **Type**: **KNOW** (Deep Dive).
- **Reasoning**: Decree 124/2021/NĐ-CP amends Decree 115, drastically **increasing fines** (up to 7x value of goods). Users aren't just looking for the document name; they want to know *what changed* and *how much they might pay* if they violate.

## 3. User Motivation
- **Core Desire**: "I need to know the new penalty levels in Decree 124 to avoid being fined heavily, especially regarding expired ingredients or lack of declaration."
- **Specific Needs**:
    - What is Decree 124 amending? (Replaces parts of Decree 115).
    - Key changes in fines (Higher? Stricter?).
    - Specific violations: Expired goods, Labeling errors, No Certificate.
    - Solution: How to not get fined?

## 4. Content Strategy
- **Recommended Format**: **Comprehensive Guide / Comparative Analysis**.
- **Structure**:
    - **Introduction**: Decree 124 - The "Heavy Hand" of the Government.
    - **Key Updates**: Compare Decree 115 vs Decree 124 (Table format is best).
    - **Top 5 Violations**: List the most common mistakes and their new fine levels.
    - **WIN Flavor Context**: Explain how R&D compliance prevents these specific fines (e.g., using "flavor with clear origin" avoids the "unverified origin" fine).
    - **CTA**: Prevention is cheaper than the fine.
- **Tone**: Warning, authoritative, urgent, but solution-oriented.

## 5. SERP Feature Predictions
- **Featured Snippet**: A table showing "Old Fine vs New Fine".
- **People Also Ask**:
    - "Nghị định 124 phạt bao nhiêu?"
    - "Điểm mới của Nghị định 124 là gì?"
    - "Xử phạt vi phạm an toàn thực phẩm mới nhất 2026".
