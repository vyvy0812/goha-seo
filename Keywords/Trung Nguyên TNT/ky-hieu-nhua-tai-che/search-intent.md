# Search Intent Analysis: Ký hiệu nhựa tái chế

> **Keyword**: Ký hiệu nhựa tái chế
> **Primary Intent**: **Informational** (Knowledge Gathering)
> **Secondary Intent**: **Commercial Investigation** (B2B Context: Manufacturers looking for standards)
> **Micro-Intent**: **Know** (Understanding the meaning of 7 plastic codes)
> **User Motivation**:
> - **End User**: Wants to identify if a plastic item is safe for health (microwave, hot water) or recyclable.
> - **B2B User**: Factory owners need to ensure their products comply with labeling standards (Decree 15/Decree 111).
> **Recommended Format**: Comprehensive Guide + Reference Table (Infographic).
> **SERP Features**: Featured Snippet (List/Table of 7 codes), Image Pack, People Also Ask.

## 7-Type Classification
1.  **Informational**: High. Users need definitions of PET, HDPE, PVC, LDPE, PP, PS, etc.
2.  **Transactional**: Low (End users don't "buy" symbols).
3.  **Commercial**: Low/Medium (B2B users looking for molds/machinery to create these symbols).
4.  **Navigational**: Low.
5.  **Brand**: Low.
6.  **News**: Low.
7.  **Local**: Low.

## Content Strategy for Trung Nguyên TNT
While the general intent is B2C (Safety/Recycling), the **Trung Nguyên TNT** angle must be **Industrial**:
*   **Hook**: Explain the symbols clearly (Traffic magnet).
*   **Bridge**: High-quality plastic products require clear, precise molding/labeling.
*   **Pitch**: PVC/Silicone Dispensing Machines (Máy chấm keo) create high-quality labels and logos that meet these standards (sharp, durable, compliant).
*   **Safety**: Emphasize that better machinery allows checking/controlling material quality (e.g., dispensing pure PVC vs. recycled contaminants).

## Implied Needs
- **Clarity**: High-resolution images of symbols.
- **Safety**: Which numbers are toxic? Which are food-safe?
- **Compliance**: How to label correctly for export/domestic sale.
