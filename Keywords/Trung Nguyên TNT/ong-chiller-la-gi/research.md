# Research: ống chiller là gì

## 1. Technical Specifications
### 1.1. Black Steel Pipes (Mainstay)
- **Standards**: 
    - **ASTM A53**: General mechanical and pressure applications (welded/seamless).
    - **ASTM A106**: High-temperature service (seamless).
    - **BS 1387 Class C / JIS G3452**: Common for smaller chilled water lines (15mm-150mm).
- **Schedules**: 
    - **SCH 40**: Standard wall thickness.
    - **SCH 80**: Extra heavy for high-pressure systems.
- **Corrosion Control**: Interior can be epoxy-coated or plastic-lined; exterior MUST be insulated to prevent condensation/rust.

### 1.2. PPR Pipes (Modern Alternative)
- **Standards**: **DIN 8077/8078**.
- **Pressure Ratings**: PN10, PN16, PN20, PN25.
- **Pros**: Zero corrosion, low heat loss (low thermal conductivity), light weight, easier connection (heat fusion).
- **Cons**: High thermal expansion coefficient (requires expansion loops/offsets), lower UV resistance.

### 1.3. Insulation (Critical Component)
- **Materials**: 
    - **Polyolefin foam**: Popular, closed-cell.
    - **Nitrile Rubber (Elastomeric)**: Brands like Armaflex, Superlon. Excellent condensation control.
    - **PU Foam (PIR/PUR)**: Highest R-value, often used in pre-insulated pipes.
- **Goal**: Prevent "sweating" when pipe temperature (7-12°C) is below ambient dew point.
