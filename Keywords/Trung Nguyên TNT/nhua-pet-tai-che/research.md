## 2. Keyword Clusters & Intent

### Cluster A: Definition & Feasibility (The "Know" Intent)
*   **Intent**: Informational (Deep Dive)
*   **Keywords**: nhựa pet tái chế (Main), quy trình tái chế nhựa pet, nhựa pet có tái chế được không, ký hiệu nhựa pet (Recycling code 1).
*   **Content Focus**: Explain PET is highly recyclable. Break down the process clearly.

### Cluster B: Market & Value (The "Commercial" Intent)
*   **Intent**: Commercial Investigation
*   **Keywords**: giá nhựa pet tái chế, giá hạt nhựa tái sinh, thị trường nhựa phế liệu.
*   **Content Focus**: Discuss economic value. Transition to **"Investment in Machinery"** to process in-house (Recycle scraps -> Save material costs).

### Cluster C: Technical Solution (The "Trung Nguyên TNT" Angle)
*   **Intent**: Transactional / Solution-Aware
*   **Keywords**: máy nghiền nhựa pet, máy sấy nhựa pet, máy móc ngành nhựa, giải pháp tự động hóa.
*   **Content Focus**:
    *   **Problem**: PET absorbs moisture -> Defects.
    *   **Solution**: **Máy sấy hút ẩm (Dehumidifier)** from Trung Nguyên TNT.
    *   **Problem**: Waste material (Scrap) -> Lost money.
    *   **Solution**: **Máy nghiền (Granulator)** to reuse sprues/runners immediately.

## 3. Strategic Recommendations
*   **The "Hygroscopic" Hook**: PET is tricky because it absorbs water. Use this to pivot to **Dehumidifying Dryers** (Máy sấy hút ẩm) - a strong auxiliary product.
*   **The "Zero Waste" Angle**: Promote **Instant Recycling** (Tái chế ngay tại máy) using beside-the-press granulators (Máy nghiền cạnh máy) + Auto-loaders (Máy hút liệu) to mix rPET with virgin material automatically. This fits the "Automation" persona perfectly.
*   **Tone**: Technical Engineer. Don't just say "recycle to save planet". Say "Recycle to reduce material cost by 20-30% active recycling of sprues/runners."
