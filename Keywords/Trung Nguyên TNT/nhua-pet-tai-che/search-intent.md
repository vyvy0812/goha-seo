# Search Intent Analysis: Nhựa PET Tái Chế

> **Keyword**: nhựa pet tái chế
> **Secondary Keywords**: quy trình tái chế nhựa pet, nhựa pet có tái chế được không, giá nhựa pet tái chế
> **Primary Intent**: Informational (Modification / Process)
> **Secondary Intent**: Commercial Investigation (Price / Machinery)
> **Micro-Intent**: Know Complex (Understanding the material & process) -> Do (Implement recycling or sourcing)
> **User Motivation**: The user (likely a manufacturer or investor) wants to understand the viability, process, and economic value of recycling PET plastic, potentially to set up a recycling line or source materials.
> **Recommended Format**: Comprehensive Technical Guide (Definitions + Process + Market/Price + Technology).
> **SERP Features**: Featured Snippets (Definitions), Video Carousels (Recycling Process), People Also Ask, Local Pack (Suppliers).

## Intent Shift for Trung Nguyên TNT
While the general intent is "Learning", the **Trung Nguyên TNT** brand filter serves the "Industrial Efficiency" intent. We must pivot the content from general "Eco-friendly" fluff to **"Industrial feasibility and automation efficiency"**.

### Content Strategy
1.  **Definition**: Clear, technical explanation of Recycled PET (rPET).
2.  **Feasibility**: Confirm recyclability with data (number of cycles, property degradation).
3.  **Process**: Detailed industrial recycling process (focus on **automation** steps: sorting, washing, crushing, pelletizing). *Opportunity to mention machinery.*
4.  **Market/Price**: Current market trends and factors affecting price.
5.  **Solution**: How automation improves rPET quality and reduces cost.
