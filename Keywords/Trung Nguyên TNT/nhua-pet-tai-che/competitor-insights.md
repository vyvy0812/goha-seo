## Strategic Analysis
*   **Common Angles**:
    *   **Definition**: All start with "What is PET?".
    *   **Safety/Reusability**: All address the "Is it safe to reuse?" question (Consumer Intent).
    *   **Identification**: How to identify PET (Symbol #1).
*   **Content Gaps**:
    *   **Industrial Depth**: Only Polystarco discusses *machines*. The others are consumer-focused (safety).
    *   **Economic Viability**: Missing detailed cost/benefit analysis for *recyclers* (not just users).
    *   **Automation**: No mention of *auto-loaders* or *dehumidifying dryers* specifically for PET (which is critical due to moisture).
*   **Structure Recommendations**:
    *   **Phase 1: Definition & Identification** (Quickly address the "What is it" and "Safety" to satisfy broad intent).
    *   **Phase 2: The Industrial Process** (Deep dive into *crushing*, *washing*, *drying* - modeled after Polystarco but more detailed).
    *   **Phase 3: The "Hidden" Challenge** (Moisture). This is where Trung Nguyên TNT sells the **Máy sấy hút ẩm**.
    *   **Phase 4: The Solution** (Full recycling line or specific machines).
