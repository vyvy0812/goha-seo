# Search Intent Analysis

> **Keyword**: so sánh các loại máy nghiền nhựa
> **Primary Intent**: Commercial (Investigation) (Confidence: High)
> **Secondary Intent**: Informational
> **Micro-Intent**: Know (Complex) / Do (Research before purchasing)
> **User Motivation**: User wants to understand the technical differences between various types of plastic shredders (low-speed vs high-speed, single-shaft vs twin-shaft) to select the optimal machine for their specific plastic recycling or production needs.
> **Recommended Format**: Comprehensive Comparison Guide / Technical Article
> **SERP Features**: Featured Snippet (List of types), Related Questions.

## Breakdown
- **Commercial Investigation**: The user is comparing options, indicating they are likely in the consideration phase of a buying journey.
- **Informational**: They need technical facts and specifications to make the comparison.
- **Brand Mapping**: As Trung Nguyên TNT, we must position our specific machines as the ideal solutions for relevant categories while providing objective, engineer-level advice.
