# Semantic Research: Các loại máy trộn nhựa

## 1. Technical Classifications & Entities

### A. Máy trộn nhựa đứng (Vertical Plastic Mixer)
- **Mechanism**: Use a central screw (vít tải) to lift materials from the bottom to the top, then disperse them in a fountain-like pattern.
- **Best for**: Plastic granules (hạt nhựa), regrinds (nhựa tái chế), masterbatch mixing.
- **Capacity**: 50kg, 100kg, 500kg, up to 10 tons per batch.
- **Material**: Inox 304/201 (Stainless steel) for anti-corrosion.
- **Entities**: Trục vít (Screw), Phễu cấp liệu (Hopper), Thời gian trộn (Mixing time: 10-20 min).

### B. Máy trộn nhựa ngang (Horizontal Plastic Mixer)
- **Mechanism**: U-shaped tank with ribbon/paddle blades (cánh trộn chữ S) rotating horizontally.
- **Best for**: High viscosity materials, mixing powder with granules, color dyeing, moist materials.
- **Pros**: High homogeneity, stable operation.
- **Entities**: Cánh trộn xoắn ốc (Ribbon blade), Motor giảm tốc (Gear motor), Thùng trộn chữ U.

### C. Máy trộn cao tốc (High-speed Mixer/Turbo Mixer)
- **Mechanism**: High RPM creating a vortex and friction heat.
- **Best for**: Masterbatch production, PVC powder mixing, intensive color distribution.
- **Entities**: Tốc độ cao (High speed), Ma sát tạo nhiệt (Friction heat), Đồng nhất tuyệt đối.

## 2. Technical Data & Physics (Gap Content)
- **Homogeneity Factors**: 
    - **Particle Shape**: Spherical vs. Irregular (Irregular is harder to mix).
    - **Bulk Density**: Light materials (PE) tend to float compared to heavy ones (PVC).
    - **RPM Impact**: Higher speed = faster dispersion but may cause material degradation if too high.
- **Efficiency Metrics**: 
    - Reducing labor: Automated feeding + Mixing (Integrated solution).
    - Power consumption: Optimization of motor kW vs. capacity.
