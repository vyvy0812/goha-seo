# Competitor Insights: Các loại máy trộn nhựa

## 1. Competitor Benchmarking

| Competitor | Focus | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|------------|-------|-----------|------------|
| **MCVN** | High Precision | Technical specs, focus on engineering plastics, detailed automation features. | Less focus on general classifications (Vertical/Horizontal). |
| **Viet Dai** | Basic Classification | Clear breakdown of Vertical vs. Horizontal, focus on Inox 304 and pricing. | Lacks deep technical physics/industrial efficiency metrics. |
| **Carno VN** | Manufacturing | Diverse models, strong branding as a manufacturer, competitive warranty. | Content is often promotional rather than value-first. |

## 2. Content Gaps & Opportunities (TNT Edge)
- **The "Automation Engineer" Voice**: While competitors sell "machines", Trung Nguyên TNT sells "efficiency". We should focus on how mixing affects the final product quality (avoiding bubbles, inconsistent color).
- **Technical Physics**: Include data on bulk density (khối lượng riêng) and particle morphology (hình thái hạt) to guide mixer selection.
- **Maintenance Authority**: Leverage the **3-6-9-12 month maintenance schedule** as a trust signal.
- **Case Studies/Economic Value**: Translate mixing speed into "Reduced Waste" and "Lower Labor Costs".

## 3. Structural Recommendations
- **Deep Comparison Table**: A matrix comparing Vertical vs. Horizontal vs. High-speed based on material type (PVC vs. PE) and capacity.
- **Selection Algorithm**: A step-by-step guide for factory owners to calculate the required capacity (Kg/h) vs. batch size.
- **Technical Data Sheets (TDS)**: Mock-up tables for machine performance.
