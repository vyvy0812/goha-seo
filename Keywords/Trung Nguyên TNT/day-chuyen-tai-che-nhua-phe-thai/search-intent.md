# Search Intent Analysis: dây chuyền tái chế nhựa phế thải

> **Keyword**: dây chuyền tái chế nhựa phế thải
> **Primary Intent**: Commercial Investigation (High Confidence)
> **Secondary Intent**: Informational (Process & Tech Specs) | Transactional (B2B Sourcing)
> **Micro-Intent**: **Do** (find a supplier/solution) + **Know** (understand the recycling process efficiency).
> **User Motivation**: The user is likely a factory owner or production manager looking for a "Turn-key" recycling solution to process plastic waste (In-house or commercial) into reusable material, aiming for cost reduction and green manufacturing.
> **Recommended Format**: **Comprehensive Technical Solution Page** (combining Process Guide + Machine Specs + ROI Analysis).
> **SERP Features**: Video (Machine operation), Image Pack (Equipment), Local Map (Supplier location).

## Insight & Strategy
- **Intent Shift**: Users are not just asking "what is it", they are asking "which system is efficient and durable?".
- **Brand Alignment**: Trung Nguyên TNT must position this as a "Green Manufacturing" solution (mentioned in Source Context), emphasizing **In-line recycling** (nghiền tái chế ngay tại chỗ) using Granulators/Crushers and Mixers. Use the "Taiwan Quality" angle for durability.
- **Content Focus**:
    1.  **Efficiency**: How the line saves labor (Automation) and energy (Servo).
    2.  **Quality**: Recycled granules must be effectively reused without degrading product quality.
    3.  **Support**: Emphasize "Lifetime Support" and "Same-day service" to address the fear of machine downtime.
