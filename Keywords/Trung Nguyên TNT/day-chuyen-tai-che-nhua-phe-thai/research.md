# Research & Semantic Analysis: dây chuyền tái chế nhựa phế thải

> **Brand Context**:
> *   **Identity**: Trung Nguyên TNT - Expert in Plastic Automation & Taiwan Tech.
> *   **Angle**: Efficiency through "In-Line Recycling" (Tái chế ngay tại chỗ). Focus on **Machine Durability** and **Automation** (Conveyors + Crushers).
> *   **Exclude**: Washing lines (unless requested). Focus on **Granulating & Mixing** (Băm & Trộn) for injection molding factories.
> *   **Target Audience**: Factory owners wanting to reuse waste runners/rejects to save material costs.

## 3. Strategic Recommendations
1.  **Redefine "Dây chuyền"**: Instead of a massive external recycling plant, define the "Line" as an **Automated Loop** within the factory: *Injection Machine -> Conveyor -> Slow Granulator -> Auto Loader -> Mixer -> Injection Machine*. This fits "Trung Nguyên TNT" perfect.
2.  **Attack the "Quality" Fear**: Address concerns that recycled plastic is weak. Counter with "Máy băm thông minh ít bụi" (Low dust) and "Máy trộn đều" to ensure product quality.
3.  **Visuals**: Use diagrams showing the "Closed Loop" workflow.
