# Competitor Insights: dây chuyền tái chế nhựa phế thải

## Top Competitors
1.  **Hợp Phát Sort**: Focuses on **Color Sorting** (Máy tách màu) and PET recycling. Very detailed on specific machines in the line (Label remover, crusher, washer, sorter). High tech focus.
2.  **Băng Tải Thành Công**: Focuses on **Washing Line** (Băm rửa) for PP/PE. Simple structure: What is it, Overview, Process, Advantages, Where to buy.
3.  **AMC**: Product page style. Focuses on Specs, Application, Diagram.

## Common Headings (Pattern)
*   **Concept**: Dây chuyền tái chế là gì? / Tổng quan.
*   **Process**: Quy trình (Băm -> Rửa -> Phân loại).
*   **Machine Types**: Máy lột, máy băm, máy rửa, máy tách màu.
*   **Application**: Nhựa PET, PP, PE.
*   **Buy/Contact**: Tại sao chọn / Mua ở đâu.

## Gap Analysis & Opportunities for Trung Nguyên TNT
*   **Missing**: "In-line Recycling" (Tái chế tại chỗ) is missing. Competitors focus on *massive washing lines* for dirty waste.
*   **Opportunity**: Trung Nguyên TNT should attack the **Factory Internal Recycling** niche. "You don't need a massive washing line if you recycle your runners immediately".
*   **Differentiation**: Focus on **Closed-loop System** (Crusher + Mixer + Auto-loader) for Injection/Blow molding factories. This is cleaner, cheaper, and fits TNT's product line (Granulators/Mixers) better than "Washing Lines" (which TNT might not prioritize).
*   **Visuals**: Diagrams of the *Closed Loop* vs the *Complex Washing Line*.
