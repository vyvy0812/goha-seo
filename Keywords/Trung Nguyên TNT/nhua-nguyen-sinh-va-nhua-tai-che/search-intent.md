# Search Intent Analysis: Nhựa Nguyên Sinh và Nhựa Tái Chế

**Keyword**: nhựa nguyên sinh và nhựa tái chế
**Primary Intent**: Informational (Confidence: High)
**Secondary Intent**: Commercial Investigation (Confidence: Medium)
**Micro-Intent**: Know
**User Motivation**: The user (likely a factory owner, purchasing manager, or student) wants to understand the fundamental differences between primary and recycled plastics to make informed decisions about product quality, pricing, and manufacturing applications.
**Recommended Format**: Comprehensive Guide / Comparison Article
**SERP Features**: Featured Snippet, Images, Related Questions (People Also Ask)

## Intent Breakdown
1.  **Informational (Know)**: Users are searching for definitions, properties, and the manufacturing process of both types of plastic.
2.  **Commercial (Investigation)**: There is a strong interest in "Comparison" (So sánh) to choose the right material for specific industrial needs (durability vs. cost).
3.  **Technical Focus**: Searches for "Nhựa nguyên sinh có tái chế được không" indicate a need for circular economy and sustainability knowledge.

## Mandatory Content Elements
- Comparison table (Properties, Price, Application).
- Physical identification guide (Color, smell, texture).
- Industrial application list (High-end vs. low-end products).
- Sustainability and recycling feasibility section.
- Technical specifications related to processing machinery (since this is for Trung Nguyên TNT).
