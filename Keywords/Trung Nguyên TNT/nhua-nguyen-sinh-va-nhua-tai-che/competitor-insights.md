## Strategic Analysis
*   **Common Angles**:
    *   Definitions of both types of plastic.
    *   Specific breakdown of PP, PE, ABS, PVC.
    *   Direct comparison table/section (Quality, Price, Application).
*   **Content Gaps**:
    *   **Automation Focus**: Competitors only mention "applications" but don't explain the *machinery* or *automation* efficiency required to process recycled plastic (e.g., filtration systems, pelletizing precision).
    *   **Technical Specs**: Lack of detailed data on melting points and density for factory owners (mostly consumer-focused).
    *   **Sustainability Logic**: None explain *why* automation is the key to making recycled plastic viable for higher-end manufacturing.
*   **Structure Recommendations**:
    *   Add a dedicated section on "Industrial Automation Solutions" for plastic processing.
    *   Include a "Technical Fact Sheet" for common polymers.
    *   Use a "Problem-Solver" approach: How to balance cost (recycled) and quality (virgin) using smart machinery.
