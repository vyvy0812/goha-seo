## 1. Technical Data: Polymers
| Polymer Type | Melting Point (°C) | Density (g/cm³) | Key Properties | Common Applications |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **PP (Polypropylene)** | 130 - 170 | 0.85 - 0.95 | High chemical resistance, food-safe, high durability. | Food containers, medical devices, car parts. |
| **PE (Polyethylene)** | 110 - 130 | 0.91 - 0.97 | Flexible, waterproof, chemical resistant. | Plastic bags, bottles, pipes. |
| **ABS (Acrylonitrile)** | 105 - 220 | 1.01 - 1.20 | IMPACT resistance, rigid, high aesthetic finish. | Electronics, toys (LEGO), household appliances. |
| **PVC (Polyvinyl)** | 100 - 260 | 1.25 - 1.50 | Durable, fire-resistant, good insulation. | Pipes, cables, flooring, shoe soles. |

## 2. Virgin vs. Recycled: Industrial Comparison
| Feature | Virgin Plastic (Nguyên sinh) | Recycled Plastic (Tái chế) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Purity** | 100% pure, no impurities. | May contain traces of other polymers/additives. |
| **Aesthetics** | Bright, smooth, transparent/clear. | Dull, darker colors (grey/black), uneven texture. |
| **Durability** | Maximum structural integrity. | 10-20% loss in tensile strength per cycle. |
| **Safety** | Food & Medical Grade certified. | Generally for industrial/household use. |
| **Automation** | Highly stable processing. | Requires precise filtration & temperature control. |

## 3. Industrial Automation & Machinery (Centro-Entity Alignment)
- **Pelletizing Lines (Máy tạo hạt nhựa)**:
    - Essential for turning recycled scrap into uniform pellets for re-use.
    - Automation focus: Twin-screw extruders for better degassing and filtration.
- **Injection Molding (Máy ép nhựa)**:
    - Clamping force: 110T to 220T standard for medium parts.
    - Precision: Automated dosing systems to maintain color consistency when using recycled material.
- **Economic Value**: Using recycled material with advanced automation can reduce raw material costs by 30-50% without compromising 90% of structural needs.
