### Machine Classification
1.  **Hopper Dryer (Sấy phễu)**: Standard hot air drying. Best for non-hygroscopic resins (PP, PE, PS).
2.  **Dehumidifying Dryer (Sấy hút ẩm)**: Uses honeycomb rotor or twin-tower molecular sieves. Essential for PET, PC, PA.
3.  **3-in-1 Compact Dryer**: Combines Dehumidifying + Drying + Conveying in one unit. Saves space and energy.
