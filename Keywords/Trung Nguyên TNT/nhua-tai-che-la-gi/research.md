## 3. Top-Level Outline Strategy (Trung Nguyên TNT Style)

1.  **Define**: Scientific definition but emphasize *economic value* (Turning trash to treasure).
2.  **Classify**: Standard 7 types. Highlight **PET** (Bottles) and **PP/PE** (Packaging) as they are most common in VN.
3.  **The "Hidden" Truth (Safety)**: Address toxicity scientifically (Migration of additives).
4.  **The Industrial Solution (Brand Connection)**:
    *   How to use recycled plastic *correctly* in production?
    *   Need **Dryers** (Hạt nhựa tái chế hút ẩm mạnh hơn nhựa zin).
    *   Need **Mixers** (Trộn đều nhựa zin và nhựa tái chế).
    *   Need **Granulators** (Tái chế tại chỗ - "Máy băm").
5.  **Conclusion**: Sustainable manufacturing is the future (Green Factory).
