# Semantic & Contextual Research: "Các loại máy sấy nhựa"

## 1. Technical Data & Live Specs
Based on SERP analysis for industrial plastic drying machines:
- **Capacities (Thể tích/Công suất chứa):** Ranges from small 5kg-8L hoppers to large 100kg+ industrial units.
- **Power (Công suất điện):** Heater power (1.2 kW to 6.5 kW) and Fan power (e.g., 245W). Three-phase power (220V/380V/440V, 50-60Hz) is standard.
- **Drying Temperatures:** Typically 40°C to 160°C, can reach up to 300°C for specific engineering plastics.
- **Core Components & Materials:** Stainless steel (SUS304) for parts contacting plastic, PID intelligent temperature controllers, double overheat protection, and specific technologies like "động cơ Nichias Nhật Bản" (Nichias Japan motors) and "rotor tổ ong rây phân tử" (honeycomb molecular sieve rotors).
- **Problems Solved:** Removes moisture to prevent major injection molding defects: bọt khí (air bubbles), vết rỗ (pockmarks), nứt vỡ (cracking), and biến dạng (warping).
