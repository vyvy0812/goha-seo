# Search Intent Analysis: Cấu Tạo Cánh Tay Robot Công Nghiệp

## 1. Primary Keyword
- **Keyword**: Cấu Tạo Cánh Tay Robot Công Nghiệp

## 2. Micro-Intent Analysis
- **Core Intent (Micro-Intent)**: Informational (Know) transitioning to Commercial Investigation (Do/Go).
- **Searcher Profile**: Technical managers, engineering students, factory owners, or procurement staff seeking to understand the anatomy of industrial robotic arms. They want to know the core components (khớp, khâu, thiết bị đầu cuối, hệ điều khiển) to evaluate quality or make maintenance/investment decisions.
- **SERP Features Triggered**: Featured Snippets (definitions/components lists), "People Also Ask" (FAQs about payload, DOF).

## 3. Formatting Recommendations
- **Content Structure**: Step-by-step breakdown of each component (cơ khí, điện, điều khiển). Bullet points for technical specs.
- **Visuals**: Diagram of a robotic arm with labeled parts. 
- **Brand Calibration (Trung Nguyên TNT)**: Must frame the explanation through the lens of **Efficiency** and **Taiwan Quality Standard** (e.g., explaining why Servo motors from Taiwan provide better ROI than cheaper alternatives).

## 4. Problem-Solution Mapping
- **Problem**: Need to understand how robotic arms work to justify the investment or maintain existing ones in plastic manufacturing.
- **Solution**: A clear, technical yet grounded guide dissecting the structure of robotic arms, highlighting how premium components result in higher efficiency (Trung Nguyên TNT's core message).
