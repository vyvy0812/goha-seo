# Competitor Insights: Cấu Tạo Cánh Tay Robot Công Nghiệp

## 1. Top 3 Competitors (Organic)
1. **servodynamics.com.vn**: Very structured, 5-part breakdown.
2. **vnatech.com.vn**: Broader focus, groups into 3 main systems.
3. **tcid.vn**: Good technical depth on joints and end-effectors.

## 2. Common Flow (The SEO Standard)
1. Definition (What is it?)
2. Detailed Anatomy / 5 Core Parts (Mechanical, Drive, Control, Sensors, End-effector).
3. Basic Technical Specs (DOF, Payload, Reach).
4. Applications/Benefits.

## 3. SEO Gaps & Opportunities for Trung Nguyên TNT
- **Gap 1: Application-Specific Durability**. Competitors talk about structures in abstract terms. They don't mention how the *materials* and *IP ratings* matter for harsh environments (like 200°C plastic injection molds).
- **Gap 2: The "Efficiency" Connection**. Competitors list parts but don't explain *why* top-tier parts matter. Trung Nguyên TNT can fill this by explaining how High-Precision Servo Motors reduce cycle times.
- **Action**: Adopt the standard 5-part flow but inject deep "Industrial Engineer" perspectives. Detail the Taiwan quality standards, emphasize Servo motor efficiency, and add a specific H2 on "Cấu tạo thiết kế chuyên dụng cho ngành nhựa".
