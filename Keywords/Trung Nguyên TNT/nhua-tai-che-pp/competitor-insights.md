## Strategic Analysis
*   **Common Angles**:
    *   **Definition**: All emphasize explaining *what* PP plastic is and if/how it can be recycled.
    *   **Process**: They cover the basic 4 steps (Collection -> Sorting -> Melting -> Pelletizing).
    *   **Applications**: Consistent mention of industrial and consumer uses.
*   **Content Gaps (Trung Nguyên TNT Opportunity)**:
    *   **Lack of Machinery Detail**: Competitors discuss "grinding" or "re-pelletizing" but don't showcase the *specific machines* (e.g., Crusher vs. Granulator). Trung Nguyên TNT can fill this gap with detailed specs.
    *   **No In-house Solution**: They focus on buying recycled pellets or general theory. Trung Nguyên TNT can offer the "Self-Recycling Line" for factories to save costs.
    *   **Missing Brand Identity**: Competitor content is generic. Trung Nguyên TNT's content will feature engineer-level precision and "Taiwan Quality" trust signals.
*   **Structure Recommendations**:
    *   **H2: Process Guide**: Adopt the step-by-step process but inject specific machinery models into each stage (e.g., Step 2: Grinding with [TNT Granulator Model]).
    *   **H2: Economic Benefits**: Add a unique section calculating ROI for in-house recycling.
    *   **H2: Technical Specs**: Include a comparison table of machine capabilities (capacity, power, material type).
