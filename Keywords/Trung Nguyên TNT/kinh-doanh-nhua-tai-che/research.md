## 3. Topical Clusters (Content Depth)

### Cluster 1: Conditions & Legal (Điều kiện)
- Business Registration (Đăng ký kinh doanh).
- Environmental Protection Commitment (Cam kết bảo vệ môi trường).
- Fire Safety (PCCC).

### Cluster 2: Setup & Machinery (Mở xưởng & Máy móc) - **High Priority**
- **Location**: Far from residential areas, power supply (3-phase).
- **Equipment List** (The "Efficiency" Angle):
    - Conveyor belts (Băng tải) -> Reduce labor.
    - **Crushers/Grinders** (Máy băm/nghiền) -> Critical for size reduction. (**Trung Nguyên TNT Focus**)
    - Washing line (Dây chuyền giặt).
    - Pelletizers (Máy tạo hạt).
    - Cooling Towers (Tháp giải nhiệt) -> Essential for machine stability. (**Trung Nguyên TNT Focus**)

### Cluster 3: Economics (Kinh tế)
- Cost of raw materials (input).
- Selling price of granules (output).
- **Operational Efficiency**: How automation (Trung Nguyên TNT machinery) reduces power/labor costs and increases margin.
